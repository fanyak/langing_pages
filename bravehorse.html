Guess What?

You are right where you're supposed to be. In this moment, you are ready to be brave.
You are ready to be you.

That is why you are here.

Welcome to Brave Horse, where the horses are the teachers. If you are ready for a
profound, life changing experience, you have come to the right place.

 

what is eal?

Equine assisted learning is your own journey. It belongs to you. it is
where the horse will teach you more about yoursef- if you are
willing. The horse know what you need. Equine assisted learning is
free from judgements and expectations.
All that is required from you is that you allow yourself to simply be, to simply experience, to
simply listen to the horse. You will take away from this experience
what you need to.
 
 

why horses

When I think of horses, two words come to mind: resilient and
wholehearted. Through history, beside every foot print made by man
there has also been a hoof pring. The horses' story has always been
entwined with ours. They are the true pioneers - event today, when
horses are no longer a necessity to survival, they still have a purpose
here. They still belong.
